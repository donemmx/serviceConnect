How Service Connect Works

A service Portal where you can have the best People for the job right now.

01
Post your Job needs as a Client

We've got you covered on Service Connect. Post your job and you'll start getting proposals. Once you've found your expert, you can talk timings, availability, and prices before going ahead.

02
Advertise Skills & Products as a Service Provider

Advertise your skills and products on Service Connect. Bid and send quotations for jobs posted by clients using our Platform.

03
Comment & Rate your favourite Service Provider

We allow clients to comment and rate their favourite service Provider. This helps to boost the profile of Service Providers and over all reliablity of the Platform.
